## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please email me at L00182257@ATU.ie to report any vulnerabilitys.
An update will first be given declaring that your notice has been recived, 
which you can expect to recive within 1-3 business days 
and if it has been accepted or declined. 

Further notice will be given after the exsploit is dealt with. 
However under servere circumstances where the issue is drastic 
and requires significant time to patch you should excpect 
to see multiple emails regarding the issue on a bi-weekly basis.
